About us

Premium Herbs & Spices Exported from Egypt to the World

Neferu Herbs is an Egyptian company specializing in the export of high-quality herbs, spices, and seeds. Founded with a passion for natural wellness and a commitment to purity, we serve clients in the food, tea, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries. Our name, Neferu, inspired by ancient Egyptian heritage, stands for beauty and excellence—values we uphold in every leaf, seed, and shipment.
